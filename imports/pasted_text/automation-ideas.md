What your bot brain really needs

Your bot should understand actions.

Core actions:

open_url
click
type
scroll
read_page
extract_data
save_file
wait_for_element

Those 8 actions can automate almost anything.

Example brain command system

Your AI thinks like this:

Goal: collect emails

Steps:
1 open google
2 search roofing companies chicago
3 open first result
4 extract email
5 save to list
6 repeat
The big secret of AI agents

Most AI agents are just:

LLM brain + simple actions

Example actions:

navigate(url)
click(selector)
type(selector, text)
scroll()
read_text()

That’s it.
