# Account, Login, and Billing

## Creating an Account

Bleumr uses a local-first profile system. When you first open the app, you create a profile that is stored on your device. No email verification or password is required to use the core features.

### Profile Information
During onboarding, you may provide:
- Full name
- Date of birth
- Email address
- Phone number
- Home address

> **Important:** This information is stored locally on your device. Bleumr does not transmit personal profile data to external servers unless you explicitly enable cloud sync. See our [Privacy and Security](/privacy-and-security) documentation for details.

### Managing Your Profile
- Open **Settings** from the sidebar.
- Select the **Profile** section to edit your information.
- You can create multiple profiles and switch between them.

The Free tier provides 15 AI calls per day using the Llama 3.1-8B model. This is suitable for casual use and exploring the platform.

The Pro tier unlocks 150 daily AI calls, access to the full Llama 3.3-70B model, and all premium features. Upgrade to Pro by purchasing a license key.

### Stellur Tier
The Stellur tier provides unlimited AI calls and priority support. Stellur is available to founding members and select partners.

## License Keys

### Activating a License Key
1. Open **Settings** from the sidebar.
2. Navigate to the **Plan** tab.
3. Enter your license key in the input field.
4. Click **Activate**.
5. Your subscription tier will update immediately.

### License Key Issues
- Each license key is tied to a single device. If you switch devices, contact support to transfer your key.
- License keys do not expire unless your subscription lapses.
- If activation fails, verify that you have an active internet connection and that the key was entered correctly (no extra spaces).

## Daily Usage Limits

- Usage limits reset at midnight UTC each day.
- You can view your remaining daily calls in the Settings panel.
- If you exceed your daily limit, you will see a message indicating that your quota has been reached. You can continue the next day or upgrade your plan.
- If the rate-limiting server is unreachable, Bleumr applies conservative local fallback limits to ensure service continuity.

## Billing and Refunds

### Payment
- License keys are purchased through the Bleumr website or authorized resellers.
- Bleumr does not store payment card information. All transactions are processed through our payment provider.

### Refund Policy
- Refund requests must be submitted within 14 days of purchase.
- To request a refund, email support@bleumr.com with your license key and reason for the request.
- Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days.
- Activated license keys that have been used extensively may not be eligible for a full refund at our discretion.

### Cancellation
- You may stop using your license key at any time. There are no recurring charges unless you have an active subscription.
- To cancel a recurring subscription, log in to your account on the Bleumr website or contact support@bleumr.com.

## Deleting Your Account

Since Bleumr uses a local-first architecture:
1. To remove your profile, open **Settings** and delete your profile.
2. To remove all local data, uninstall the Bleumr application. This deletes all conversations, settings, and cached data from your device.
3. If you have enabled cloud sync, contact support@bleumr.com to request deletion of your synced data.
