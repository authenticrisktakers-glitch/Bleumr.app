# Calendar and Scheduler

## Overview

Bleumr includes a built-in calendar for scheduling events, blocking time, and organizing your day. You can create events manually or ask JUMARI to schedule them through conversation.

## Features

### Calendar Views
- **Monthly View**: See your entire month at a glance with event indicators on each day.
- **Weekly View**: Detailed view of your week with time slots.

### Creating Events
1. Click on a date or time slot in the calendar.
2. Enter the event title, start time, end time, and optional notes.
3. Choose a color to categorize the event.
4. Click Save.

Alternatively, ask JUMARI: "Schedule a meeting with the design team for Thursday at 2 PM."

### Managing Events
- **Edit**: Click an existing event to modify its details.
- **Delete**: Click the delete button on an event to remove it.
- **Color Coding**: Assign colors to categorize events (work, personal, health, etc.).

### Navigation
- Use the forward and back arrows to navigate between months.
- Click "Today" to jump to the current date.

## Limitations

- The calendar is local to your Bleumr installation. Events are stored on your device and are not synced to external calendar services (Google Calendar, Outlook, Apple Calendar).
- Calendar events do not trigger system notifications or alarms. They serve as a visual reference within Bleumr.
- If you uninstall Bleumr, calendar data is deleted along with other local data.

---

*The Calendar is designed as a quick scheduling reference within Bleumr. For comprehensive calendar management with reminders, invitations, and cross-device sync, we recommend using a dedicated calendar application alongside Bleumr.*
