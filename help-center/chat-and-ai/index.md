# Chat and AI

## Overview

The chat interface is the heart of Bleumr. It provides a conversational AI assistant called JUMARI that can answer questions, search the web, analyze images, generate content, write code, and much more. JUMARI uses large language models to understand your messages and provide helpful, contextual responses.

## Starting a Conversation

1. Click the chat input at the bottom of the main screen.
2. Type your message and press Enter or click Send.
3. JUMARI will respond with a streaming reply that appears in real time.

## Features

### Web Search
JUMARI can search the web to provide up-to-date information. Simply ask a question that requires current data, and JUMARI will automatically search and cite its sources.

- Web search uses DuckDuckGo and does not require an additional API key.
- Search results are summarized and presented inline in the conversation.
- Sources are cited so you can verify the information.

### Image Analysis
Upload an image by dragging and dropping it into the chat, or by clicking the attachment icon.

- JUMARI can describe, analyze, and answer questions about uploaded images.
- Supported formats: JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP.
- Image analysis counts as one AI call against your daily limit.

### Thread Management
Conversations are organized into threads for easy reference.

- **New Thread**: Click the "+" button to start a fresh conversation.
- **Thread History**: Previous threads are listed in the sidebar, grouped by date (Today, Yesterday, Last 7 Days, Older).
- **Search Threads**: Use the search function to find past conversations by keyword.
- **Delete Thread**: Right-click or use the menu on a thread to delete it. Deleted threads cannot be recovered.

### Follow-up Suggestions
After JUMARI responds, you may see suggested follow-up questions. Click any suggestion to continue the conversation in that direction.

### Message Feedback
Use the thumbs-up or thumbs-down buttons on any JUMARI response to provide feedback. This helps improve the quality of future responses.

## AI Models

Bleumr uses the following AI models through the Groq API:

| Model | Availability | Best For |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| Llama 3.1-8B Instant | Free, Pro, Stellur | Quick responses, simple tasks |
| Llama 3.3-70B Versatile | Pro, Stellur | Complex reasoning, detailed analysis, creative writing |

- The model used depends on your subscription tier and the complexity of the request.
- You can configure your preferred model in **Settings > Engine**.

## Memory and Personalization

JUMARI has a memory system that remembers key information across conversations:

- **Automatic Memory**: JUMARI may automatically remember important details you share (preferences, goals, facts about you) to provide more personalized responses in future conversations.
- **Memory Categories**: Preferences, facts, goals, context, and skills.
- **Memory Limit**: Up to 120 memories are stored, with automatic deduplication.
- **Memory is Local**: Memory data is stored on your device and is not shared with other users.
- **Clearing Memory**: You can ask JUMARI to forget specific information, or clear all memory from Settings.

## Limitations and Disclaimers

- JUMARI is an AI assistant and may produce inaccurate, incomplete, or outdated information. Always verify important information from authoritative sources.
- JUMARI does not have access to real-time data unless it performs a web search.
- Responses are generated by large language models and do not constitute professional advice (medical, legal, financial, or otherwise).
- Conversation content is processed through the Groq API. While Bleumr takes measures to protect your data, messages are transmitted to third-party servers for processing. See our [Privacy and Security](/privacy-and-security) documentation for details.
